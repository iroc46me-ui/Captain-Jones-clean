# Harbor Bible

## Core Brand Feeling
A cinematic harbor of hidden value. Visitors should feel: “I want to explore this harbor.”

## Brand Archetype
Keeper of forgotten treasure, not cartoon pirate chaos.

## Tone
Mysterious, trustworthy, atmospheric, mature, seller-friendly, discovery-oriented.

## Visual Palette
Deep ocean cyan, midnight blue, lantern amber, muted gold, weathered brass.

## Marketplace Promise
Lower-fee, story-driven, discovery-first marketplace for useful junk, rare finds, antiques, gold/prospecting gear, tools, oddities, RV finds, handmade goods, and collectibles.
